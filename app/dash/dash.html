About the Tricktionary

The Tricktionary is a dictionary of tricks and a swiss army knife for any rope skipping competition offering live speed scoring as well as well as a way to manage your competition in a comfortable way (beta)

The tricktionary is currently available as an Android™ app and as this webapp, but don't worry, an iOS™ app is comming right up.

Explanation of Levels

On some occurrences you might notice levels with a dash between two or more numbers, this means that the trick is actually a composition of multiple tricks, the first digit is the level of the first occuring trick followed by a dash and the level of the second trick, and so on.

A typical example of this is a sandard Pushup which in fisac-irsf's rulebook is split into two separate tricks, one where you jump into the pushup, and one when you jump out from it.
